# Privacy Policy for Prompt Harvest

**Last updated: April 4, 2026**

## Overview
Prompt Harvest is a Chrome extension that extracts AI prompts from YouTube video transcripts using Claude AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
- **Anonymous user ID**: A randomly generated UUID stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local). This is used solely to track your monthly free extraction count. It is never linked to your name, email, or any personal information.
- **Extraction history**: Titles and extracted prompts from videos you process are stored locally on your device only. We never upload your history to any server.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
- We do not track which YouTube videos you visit.
- We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data.
- We do not use cookies or third-party analytics.

## Third-Party Services
- **Anthropic Claude API**: Transcript text is sent to our proxy server, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API to extract prompts. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- **YouTube**: The extension reads transcript data from YouTube pages you are actively viewing. No data is sent to YouTube on your behalf.

## Data Storage
All personal data (user ID, history) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension or clearing Chrome's extension storage.
